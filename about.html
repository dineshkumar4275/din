How It Began

Founded in 2015 by fashion enthusiast Sarah Johnson, Gowear started as a small boutique in downtown Seattle. What began as a single rack of handpicked dresses has grown into a beloved local destination for women seeking unique, high-quality fashion.

Sarah's vision was simple: create a space where every woman could find clothing that made her feel confident and beautiful, without compromising on quality or breaking the bank. Today, that vision lives on through our carefully curated collections and personalized styling services.

Our Philosophy

At Gowear, we believe fashion should be joyful, accessible, and sustainable. We're committed to:

  • Curating dresses that combine quality craftsmanship with timeless design
  • Supporting local designers and ethical manufacturers
  • Providing personalized styling advice to help you discover your signature look
  • Creating a welcoming space where all women feel celebrated

We're not just selling dresses - we're helping you express your unique story through fashion.
